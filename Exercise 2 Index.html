What is Squads

Squad is a realism-based military tactical first-person shooter video game developed and published by Canadian indie developer Offworld Industries exclusively through the Steam distribution platform.It is a spiritual successor to the Project Reality modification for Battlefield 2.

Who are we?

We are a small local group dedicated to creating a space for playing sqauds in the ways it is intended to be played with strict adherance to its core principles of teamwork, coordination, and communication. We believe in small, close-knit communities rather than large communities where you never know anyone. The community being small and tight-packed means you will always feel right at home when playing, as you play with the same squad mates as before, allowing you to improve not only yourself but your teammates through coordination and learning to cover each other weaknesses.
